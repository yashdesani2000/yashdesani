Contractor Bookkeeping Services

At Opex Bookkeeping, we provide specialized accounting services for contractors and construction professionals. Our goal is to allow you to focus on your projects while we manage your bookkeeping and financial tasks with precision.

While the core accounting needs of contractors are similar to those in other fields, there are unique challenges related to project management, job costing, and compliance. Partnering with a team experienced in contractor accounting ensures that your financials are managed efficiently. At Opex Bookkeeping, we understand the complexities associated with project expenses, subcontractor payments, and industry-specific financial management.

  • Outsourced accounting and bookkeeping
  • Project and job costing
  • Contractor-specific financial management
  • Cash flow management
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Financial reporting
