Opex Bookkeeping Services Pricing

Opex Bookkeeping offers comprehensive online bookkeeping services, including transaction categorization, monthly reconciliations, and more. As a QuickBooks Proadvisor, we provide bookkeeping services online, matching the efficiency of an in-house bookkeeper. Note that we do not offer tax filing or payroll processing services at this time.

Our Services

Special Offer for Small Businesses

If you are a small business owner with 50-200 monthly transactions, we offer fixed pricing to simplify your decision. For businesses with more than 200 transactions, request a free quote to receive the most competitive price tailored to your needs.
